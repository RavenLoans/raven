**Palette (cool pearl-violet — raven oil-slick feather):**
- Paper `#eef0f6` · panel `#fbfcff` · panel2 `#e6e8f2`
- Ink `#151625` (cool near-black) · sub `#48485f` · mut `#7a7b95` · dim `#a4a4bd`
- **Iridescence:** teal `#12b39a` → violet `#6a4be0` → magenta `#c74dea` (the signature gradient, always this order)
- Amber `#cf7a2c` (liq/attention — deliberately NOT Magpie gold) · red `#c93b52` (danger)

**Type:** Fraunces (display, serif — big high-contrast headlines) · Inter (UI/body) · JetBrains Mono (data, chips, eyebrows).

**Mark:** a bird-head profile facing left — heavy wedge beak, sleek crown, iridescent gradient fill. SVG `raven()` in `_studio/build.js` (single closed path + eye). Also `ravenFly()` for small corner/watermark use.

**Voice:** confident, terse, doctrine over benefit. *"Any bag that trades is a credit line."* · *"Collateral that can still sell itself."* · *"The raven remembers."* Cool iridescence, never warm gold. The trust pitch is the product: web-first, no keys held, receipts for everything, and one word that says it all — **0 signatures ever asked.**

**Domain:** `ravenloans.xyz` (live) · **Ticker:** `$RAVEN`. Only the X handle is still open — no asset hardcodes it, so nothing needs re-rendering when it's set.
